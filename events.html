Fun Events

Here at Arendal Interactive Science Museum we have allot of events that go on all the time, from guest lecturers, build your own trebuchet and loaned exhibitions on jet engines. Please do come and take a look, there is plenty to learn and even more to explore!

It is our pleasure to announce that Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting ‘space’.
